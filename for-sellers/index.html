Why Sell on MAKKET?

Reach more buyers — Thousands of buyers search MAKKET daily. Your stall becomes visible to people across all 3 launch cities.
Skip the queue — No waiting for foot traffic. Buyers find you through search and place orders directly.
Get paid safely — Every transaction is protected by escrow-backed payment processing through licensed partners. You get paid once the buyer confirms delivery.
Build your reputation — Your Trust Score (0–100) grows with every successful sale. Higher scores mean more visibility.
Free to list — Creating your MAKKET seller profile and listing products costs nothing.
Doorstep delivery handled — MAKKET coordinates delivery so you can focus on your business.

How It Works

  1. Register & Verify — Sign up, verify your phone number, and tell us which market you sell in. Our field agents will visit your stall to complete verification.
  2. List Your Products — Add photos, descriptions, and prices for your products. The better your listings, the higher you rank in search.
  3. Receive Orders & Get Paid — When a buyer places an order, you confirm it, package the item, and hand it off for delivery. Payment hits your account once the buyer confirms receipt.

Verification Tiers

  • Listed — Phone verified. You can list products and receive orders.
  • Verified — Field agent visited your stall. Badge appears on your profile. More buyer trust.
  • Premium — Top-tier sellers. Priority search ranking, premium badge, highest buyer confidence.
