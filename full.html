The Challenge

Africa's biggest economy runs on trust you can't see

Over 80% of commerce in Nigeria happens in physical markets. Yet there's no way to search, compare, or verify before you go.

Hours lost to traffic & walking

Buyers spend 2-4 hours per trip — battling traffic, hopping between stalls, comparing prices blindly, only to leave empty-handed.

No visible trust signals

There are no ratings, no reviews, no verification. You can't tell who's reliable until you've already paid — or been burned.

Discovery depends on luck

Finding the right seller means word-of-mouth, WhatsApp broadcasts, or physically walking market rows. There's no search engine for markets.

How It Works

From search to doorstep in 5 simple steps

MAKKET gives you the quality and prices of physical markets — without the stress of going there yourself.

1

Search for what you need

Type any product — tomatoes, ankara, spare parts, cement. MAKKET finds it in the closest mapped markets near you.

2

Browse verified sellers nearby

See trusted sellers in your nearest markets — with ratings, reviews, and field-verified badges. Compare prices and quality before you commit.

3

Order with live confirmation

Select the best-value goods and receive live photos from the seller confirming current stock and freshness. What you see is what you get.

4

Confirm & pay securely

Review your order, confirm the total, and pay securely through the app. Your money is held safely until you receive your goods.

5

Get it delivered to you

A dispatch rider picks up your order from the market and delivers it straight to your door or chosen pickup point.
