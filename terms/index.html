4. Transaction model

4.1 Transaction-first approach

MAKKET operates a transaction-first model. Buyers and sellers do not exchange contact information before a purchase is completed. Seller contact details are revealed to the buyer only after purchase, solely for legitimate dispute resolution.

4.2 Escrow payments

All payments are held in escrow by MAKKET until the buyer confirms receipt and satisfaction with the order. Funds are released to the seller (minus applicable commission) after confirmation or after the dispute resolution window closes.

4.3 Commission

MAKKET charges a commission on completed transactions. Current commission rates are displayed at checkout. Sellers are informed of the net payout before accepting an order.

5. Seller verification

Sellers on MAKKET are subject to a 3-tier verification process:

  1. Listed — basic profile with phone verification.
  2. Verified — physical market visit by MAKKET field agents to confirm stall existence, trader identity, and inventory.
  3. Premium — verified plus customer rating thresholds, response-rate standards, and escrow-secured transactions.

Verification status and Trust Scores are provided for informational purposes. MAKKET does not guarantee the quality, safety, or legality of any product listed on the Platform.
