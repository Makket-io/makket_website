Seller Support

We're here to help
your business grow

From setting up your profile to getting paid — everything a market seller needs to succeed on MAKKET.

Getting Started

You can sign up through the app yourself, or request a field agent visit. Our agents come to your shop, take photos, verify your identity, and set up your complete profile in under 10 minutes. There's no cost for this.

For basic listing: a phone number and business name. For full verification: our field agent visits your shop to confirm your location, take photos, and verify your identity. This earns you the "Verified by MAKKET" badge that buyers trust.

Yes, a basic Android phone (Android 8 or newer) is needed. The app is designed to work on low-end devices and with limited internet. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use MAKKET.

Orders & Payments

When a buyer orders from you, you'll get a notification. You confirm the order, prepare the items, and send live photos to the buyer. Once they confirm, a dispatch rider picks up from your stall and delivers to the buyer.

Your payout is processed within 24 hours of delivery confirmation. The 5% platform commission is deducted automatically. Funds go to your registered bank account or mobile money wallet.

MAKKET charges 5% on each sale made through the platform. This covers platform maintenance, buyer acquisition, payment processing, and support. If you don't make sales through the app, you pay nothing.

Growing Your Business

Keep your product listings updated with clear photos and accurate prices. Respond quickly to orders — sellers with fast response times rank higher in search. Collect good reviews by delivering quality goods. You can also upgrade to Premium for priority placement.

Premium sellers pay ₦5,000/month for priority search placement, a gold badge, and enhanced profile features. To qualify, you need 3+ months of 3.5+ ratings, 80%+ response rate, and at least 10 verified reviews.
