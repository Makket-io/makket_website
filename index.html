The Challenge

Africa's biggest economy runs on trust you can't see

Over 80% of commerce in Nigeria happens in physical markets. Yet there's no way to search, compare, or verify before you go.

🚗

Hours lost to traffic & walking

Buyers spend 2-4 hours per trip — battling traffic, hopping between stalls, comparing prices blindly, only to leave empty-handed.

😶

No visible trust signals

There are no ratings, no reviews, no verification. You can't tell who's reliable until you've already paid — or been burned.

🔍

Discovery depends on luck

Finding the right seller means word-of-mouth, WhatsApp broadcasts, or physically walking market rows. There's no search engine for markets.
