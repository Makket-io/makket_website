Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 10, 2026. Last updated: April 10, 2026.

MAKKET ("we", "us", "our") operates the website https://www.makket.io and the MAKKET mobile application (together, the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Platform.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account data — name, phone number, email address, city, and role (buyer, seller, or rider) when you join the waitlist or create an account.
  • Seller profile data — market name, stall location, product categories, business name, and identity documents submitted for verification.
  • Transaction data — order details, payment information, delivery addresses, and communication related to orders.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device data — device type, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, search queries, and interaction timestamps.
  • Location data — approximate location (city-level) derived from IP address. Precise GPS location is collected only with your explicit consent and only while the app is in use.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Platform.
  • To verify seller identity and stall existence through our 3-tier verification process.
  • To process transactions and facilitate escrow payments.
  • To connect buyers with relevant verified sellers based on product searches and location.
  • To send transactional notifications (order updates, payment confirmations, delivery status).
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent; you can opt out at any time).
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With transaction counterparties — after a purchase is completed, limited seller contact information is shared with the buyer for legitimate dispute resolution. Buyer delivery details are shared with the assigned rider.
  • With service providers — payment processors, cloud hosting providers, analytics tools, and SMS/notification services that help us operate the Platform.
  • For legal compliance — when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • For safety — to protect the rights, property, or safety of MAKKET, our users, or the public.

4. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 6 years for regulatory compliance. You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

7. Cookies and tracking

The Platform uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We use analytics tools to understand usage patterns. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
